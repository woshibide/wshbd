Gilles Deleuze’s text, paired with Francis Bacon’s haunting paintings in one book

Philosophy meets art on the margins, where Francis Bacon’s paintings sit side by side with Gilles Deleuze’s dense exploration of sensation. The visual integration of art with text adds another layer to the experience—complex thoughts made a little harder to digest, thanks to the emotional weight of the imagery. An intellectual feast, paired with some haunting visuals, making the margins just as important as the main text
