A book exploring how vintage clothing carries memory and identity

Author explores the intricate relationship between memory and fashion, examining how vintage clothing serves as a vessel for nostalgia and personal history. It touches onto the ways material objects become carriers of remembrance, particularly in the context of immigration, where garments can hold the weight of past identities and lost places.
