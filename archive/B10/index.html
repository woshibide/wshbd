A fresh visual identity for Enschede’s green campaign

Enschede’s going green, and this campaign is the visual identity to match. Tote bags double as eco-billboards, encouraging residents to live greener without being preachy. The design is practical, eye-catching, and somehow turns sustainability into something cool. It’s not just about saving the planet; it’s about making green living look good on you
