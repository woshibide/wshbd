A typography-driven editorial proposal for Rewire festival

The Rewire Festival proposal takes typography and lets it run wild. No images, just letters doing what they do best—making noise, creating chaos, and somehow still staying in sync with the festival’s eclectic spirit. The design feels like a visual manifestation of experimental sound, with text that’s always on the edge of breaking free from the page. It’s as avant-garde as the festival itself, and twice as daring.
