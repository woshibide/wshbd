An interactive web-essay exploring "Passage to the Forest"

What if reading wasn’t linear? This web-essay takes Ernst Jünger’s "Passage to the Forest" and turns it into an interactive experience where words shift, stack, and make the reader pause. Here, reading becomes an adventure—a hike through the woods of meaning where nothing stays still for too long. It’s a digital forest, and you’re the explorer, zooming in, stepping back, and losing yourself in the process.
