I Am My Lover: Photoshoots Against the Backdrop of Oral History

Susanne Snyder talks with Joani Blank and Leigh Davidson about how they used publishing as a tool for sex liberation—doing it their own way, on their own terms. Their work pushed back against the flood of mainstream porn, offering something different, something personal. This magazine is a nod to them—two key voices in feminist self-publishing, who printed what they believed in when it wasn’t easy to do so.
