A logo animation celebrating 450 years of Leiden University

For Leiden University’s 450th anniversary, the logo animation brings its history to life. It flows between centuries, effortlessly blending the past and the future in a visual rhythm that mirrors the university’s journey. More than a static symbol, it captures a sense of movement, showing that even after 450 years, this institution is anything but stuck in time
