Pyotr Goloub is a designer who uses programming to design. Based in The Hague, he works across borders, seeking harmony between modern aesthetics and functional design. His work revolves around idea of anti-disciplinary approach, with a special passion for editorial and web projects.

Tailored project selection and CV are available upon a request. It is now MM:HH:SS in Netherlands, lets bring the highest values to earth by means of our labour.
