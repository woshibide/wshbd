hello

wshbd is a design studio that loves making websites and books; cross-disciplinary practice at its core means blending diverse fields to create unique, concept-driven work that challenges conventions and invites new perspectives. The mission? To create by their labor the highest values on earth.

services

A toolbox of services built to handle just about anything: Editorial, Graphic, Motion, UX/UI, Digital, Spatial, and Web design. Ideation and Concept? Always on the menu. Creative Direction? No problem. Whether it’s Websites, Signage & Wayfinding, Guidelines, or Internal Brand Building, the gears are always turning. Private commissions (if you’ve spotted some odd indexing in the archive, it’s locked behind NDAs), public commissions, or something that exists in the space between—everything’s in play. And if the job calls for extra hands, there’s a network of specialists ready to jump in. Collaboration is where the magic happens.

media

For media inquiries.. just like text? No need for formalities. High-res images, interviews, or just some inside info? It’s all available.
