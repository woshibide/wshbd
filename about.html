about

wshbd is a design studio that loves making websites and books; cross-disciplinary practice at its core means blending diverse fields to create unique, concept-driven work that challenges conventions and invites new perspectives.

This design studio is a one-man orchestra, led by Pyotr Goloub. Every project is an experiment, a challenge to the status quo, constantly pushing the boundaries of what design can (and maybe shouldn’t) do. It’s not about reinventing the wheel—it’s about wondering if we even needed one to begin with. The mission? To create by their labor the highest values on earth.

services

A toolbox of services built to handle just about anything: Editorial, Graphic, Motion, UX/UI, Digital, Spatial, and Web design. Ideation and Concept? Always on the menu. Creative Direction? No problem. Whether it’s Websites, Signage & Wayfinding, Guidelines, or Internal Brand Building, the gears are always turning.

Private commissions (if you’ve spotted some odd indexing in the archive, it’s either locked behind NDAs or, let’s be honest, too embarrassing to put on display), public commissions, or something that exists in the space between—everything’s in play. And if the job calls for extra hands, there’s a network of specialists ready to jump in. Collaboration is where the magic happens, and clients? They’re co-conspirators, not bystanders.

careers

Currently, there are no open positions—internships included. But talent never goes unnoticed. Always on the lookout for people ready to embrace challenges, especially those with a sense of humor and a taste for the absurd.
